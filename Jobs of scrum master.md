# Jobs of scrum master

- Hold and coordinate daily scrum meeting
   - Not *managing nor leading* anything, team is self managing 
   - Only micro-managing if needed
- Organize sprint planning meetings and retrospective
   - Get the cinnamon buns & hot coffee 
- Enforcer of rules set previously by team or scrum process  
- Urges team to interact and discuss issues
- Act as buffer between team and any interfering source
   - Acting as the sheep dog
- Master of the makeshift planning board with loads of stickies on it
